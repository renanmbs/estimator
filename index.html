Custom Z Clip Configurator

Configurator Guide

📝 Basics

  • Fill in Z Clip Type, Hole Size, Quantity, Length, and Spacing.
  • Click Add New Option to include more Z Clip options within the order.
  • Click CALCULATE to generate results and a PDF drawing.

⚙️ Length & Optimization

  • Increment: Lengths must be in 0.125" steps.
  • Optimize: Use this to account for saw blade thickness (0.125").
  • Red: Optimization ON.
    Black: Optimization OFF.

Holes & Lead-In

  • Spacing: Must be in 0.5" increments and smaller than total length.
  • Lead-In: Minimum lead-in is 0.5". The system automatically rounds to the nearest 1/16".

You can add notes to the Additional Notes section below the form. Which will be included in the PDF.

Please note that all measurements are in inches
